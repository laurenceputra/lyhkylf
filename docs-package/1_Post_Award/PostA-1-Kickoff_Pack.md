# Appendix A. Templatised messages (optional, copy/paste)

## A1. Caregiver outreach message (WhatsApp/SMS)
Hi [Name], this is [Charity]. Your child has been referred for [Program]. The program provides [brief description]. Participation is voluntary. We will collect minimal data to run the program and you may opt out of non-essential surveys. If you are interested, reply YES and we will share onboarding details and the consent form.

## A2. Vendor / partner message (attendance or participation confirmation)
Hi [Vendor/Partner], we would like to sponsor sessions for [Participant ID only] via our program. We will pay you directly where applicable. We require a simple participation confirmation each month (template provided). Please confirm you can provide this and share a contact for any child-safety concerns.

## A3. Monthly check-in script (for staff)
1) Last month: what did you do and why?  
2) Did you attend/participate as planned? If not, what got in the way?  
3) What helped? What should we change?  
4) Next month: what will you do? What is your plan to follow through (time/transport/buddy)?  
5) Record rubric score and next steps.
